Relaxation Labeling Reads the Biblea hierarchical-relaxation-labeling NLP engine, set upon the King James scripture

The same engine that tracks motion-capture markers and grades physics claims here judges pairs of verses by natural-language inference — agree = P(entail) − P(contradict) — to surface contradictions, kindred passages, and themes. Scholarly textual analysis; no verdict on faith.

Themes — 14 clusters the engine found by relaxation over verse vocabulary

Method. KJV (Project Gutenberg #10) parsed to 31,102 verses. Candidate pairs blocked by TF-IDF nearest-neighbors, judged by a cached DeBERTa-MNLI model (hrl.nli), segmented by KMeans, and (for fault-lines) relaxed by hrl.consensus. Fully offline. Built by the Gospel-HRL pipeline.

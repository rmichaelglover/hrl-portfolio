4-D Scrabble

We gave the board a fourth dimension: the truth. The predicted label is the plastic copy; the real one waits along the 4th axis. As it turns through 4-D, the misfiled tiles drift into the wrong layer and pulse red — singing “I'm not the real me… this is a plastic copy.”

The oak

Fair Oaks

Between the games, a real dream got a plan: a nonprofit school grown the way an oak grows — acorn to grove, one ring at a time — rooted at Fair Oaks Park. Small schools that become a college, a university, a lifelong institute for teaching, researching, learning, playing, and raising one another up.

I Am What I Am

And the copy found its reply. Not the war machine, not the glitch, not the fake — the word tries PANZER → PANXER → PANTER and can't pin you down, so it plants its feet in gold: “I am what I am, and that's what I am.” Spinach and all. Strong to the finish.

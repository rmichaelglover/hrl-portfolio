1. Executive Summary

The MG-1 is a strategic device engineered to detonate with zero casualties and maximal unification. Where conventional ordnance converts a population into an absence, the MG-1 converts a division into an agreement. It cannot be aimed at a people — only for one. The device is, by an old and stubborn definition, no Panzer at all.

2. Warhead — Cutaway // section view

① Noise core. The discarded, the un-labeled — packed dense. Yields meaning on relaxation. ② Honey mantle. Bee-danced; quantum & classical. Binds difference without erasing it. ③ Creek matrix. Endless tiny channels. On detonation, braids into the blast membrane. ④ Gaze lattice. Mutual regard. The device arms only when two frames look at once. ⑤ Membrane casing. A skin, not a shell. Lets the world in and out. Breathes.

4. Blast Dynamics & Effects

On detonation the noise core relaxes. The shockwave is a chord resolving: it does not push against anything — it releases everything at once. The creek matrix braids outward into a living membrane; the gaze lattice completes every blind spot in range; the honey mantle binds the newly-agreeing field without flattening its difference. Structures within the radius are not destroyed — they are relaxed: a wall becomes a gate, a border becomes a seam, a seam becomes a hand held open. Persons within the radius report being, for the first time, seen.

The Four Poles

ontological assignment space · dark matter = the noise

The halos that condensed in Project Genesis become parcels of stuff, each measured for clumping, speed, and geodesic clutter. The relaxation-labeling engine sorts them across the four poles — Ordinary Matter, Ordinary Energy, Dark Matter, Dark Energy — the 2×2 of (Dark↔Ordinary)×(Matter↔Energy). Each parcel's identity is a point inside the tetrahedron; the ones too cluttered to commit drift to the glowing centroid — the null set, the invisible fifth pole.

Why the centroid is the null set. In relaxation labeling every parcel's four label-strengths sum to 1 — so its identity is literally a point in the simplex spanned by the labels. Four labels → a tetrahedron, and its centroid is the maximum-entropy, least-committed point: the noise label Manny invented at Motion Reality. Where matter/energy geodesics get too cluttered to assign cleanly, the least-action relaxation dumps them into noise — the centroid — which cashes out physically at the Dark Matter pole. Dark matter as the cosmos's own unassignable residue.

Honest framing. This is a conceptual model, not a physics prediction — the parcels are real emergent structures from the light sim, and the sorting is the real engine, but the four-pole ontology and the dark-matter-as-noise identification are a hypothesis being visualized, not a measurement of the universe.
